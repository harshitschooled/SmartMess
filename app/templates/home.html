The Wastage Problem

College kitchens cook blindly for thousands of students daily, resulting in tons of excess food prep and immense waste. SmartMess eliminates guessing.

Eating or Skipping?

Students confirm their attendance for Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner before strict daily deadlines. The kitchen only prepares food for registered students.

Analytics & Logging

Admins monitor attendance trends, log daily prep and consumption in kilograms, evaluate food waste percentages, and collect student quality feedback.

How It Works

1
Register Account

Students sign up with their college email address to gain access to booking portals.

2
Mark Bookings

Mark "Eating" or "Skipping" before local kitchen deadlines close.

3
Kitchen Cooks

Kitchen staff prepares exact proportions based on backend booking counts.

4
Submit Feedback

Students rate their meals and help administrators maintain taste and hygiene standards.
